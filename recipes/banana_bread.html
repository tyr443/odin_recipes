Banana bread

Source: The Nordic Baking Book by Magnus Nilsson

A delicious banana bread with a hint of coffee.

  • 150g butter (room temperature)
  • 270g sugar
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 300g weak wheat flour
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda (baking soda)
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 4 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 50ml cold coffee
  1. Preheat the oven to 175Â°C. Butter a 24-cm cake pan
  2. Place the butter and sugar in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment and beat at medium speed until pale and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs, one by one, making sure each is fully incorporated before you add the next.
  4. Sift all of the dry ingredients together into the bowl and mix gently until just combined.
  5. Add the mashed bananas and the coffee and stir until everything is just combined.
  6. Scrape the batter into the prepared cake pan, smooth the surface and bake for 40 minutes. The cake should be quite moist.
