A base indian curry paste to mix with meats or as a spread.

  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 100g red onion, pureed
  • 1 tsp garlic & ginger paste
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tbsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • Water, as needed
  • 200g tomatoes, pureed
  1. In a pan over medium heat, melt 1 tbsp ghee.
  2. Stir in the pureed onion and garlic & ginger paste. Cook for 3–4 minutes, stirring, until browned.
  3. Add the turmeric, cumin, coriander, and Kashmiri red chilli powder. Mix well. Add a splash of water if needed to prevent sticking. Cook for about 1 minute.
  4. Stir in the pureed tomatoes. Cook for ~5 minutes, until the oil begins to separate.
