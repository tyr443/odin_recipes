Quick, low sugar, high protein over night oats recipe

  • 35g oats
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp shredded coconut
  • 18g protein powder
  • 120ml oat milk
  • 40g frozen berries
  1. Mix together the oats, chia seeds, coconut, and protein powder in a bowl or container.
  2. Add the oat milk and stir thoroughly with a fork until no lumps or dry spots remain.
  3. Fold in the frozen berries.
  4. Store covered in the fridge—ready to eat chilled, or let it sit for at least 15–30 minutes for best texture.
